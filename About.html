Director - Emily Johnston

Emilys classes are inclusive, judgement free, and are directed towards finding your confidence. Her background and training allow for detailed instruction on muscle isolations and flow arts.

Bio

Emily has been dancing or performing her entire life with a huge love for stage and screen. Inspired by her grandmothers belly dance performances on Denman island, she began her professional journey of learning and performing Egyptian style belly dance at the age of 16 in Vancouver, BC. She has attended workshops and private lessons by various world class or award winning teachers. With an extensive history of dance experience and a huge passion for latin, Emily has recently branched her performing and teaching into Salsa and Bachata Sensual, in hopes, to add to the already enriched latin dance community growing in the Comox Valley.

Certifications

  • Degree in performing arts (with distinction) Capilano University
  • Fitness theory certification - BCRPA
  • Shimmy mob leader
  • Experience of note: White rock sea festival, Surrey mayfair, Shimmy Mob lead Langley, Taboo tradeshow, Triple Soul troupe, "Bellys for boobies" fundraiser choreographer
