Classes

Egyptian Bellydance

Beginner, Intermediate and Advanced training. Choreography, layering, stage presence and deliberate training are all taught in class.

Latin Ladydance

Body Isolations, arm styling, footwork and choreography. All the components you need broken down to really define your style in Salsa or Bachata.

Bellydance Props

Veils, sword, zills and isis wings are the props we visit in this class. Basics of how to hold, move and layer belly dance moves to your new flow art.

Bachatta Sensual

Bachata sensual incorporates slow flowy movements, and body isolations. This makes for a very romantic partner dance. Footwork warm up, choreography breakdown and social dance are brought to attention in this class. Intentions are to enjoy your time and your partner here.

Fitness for Dancers

This intensive is for toning, flexibility and stamina. We work all the zones a dancer needs to improve to hit those high kicks, splits, and back bends. Warmup, cardio, weights and (of course) dancing. No choreography, this is a fitness class designed for social dancers looking to level up or maintain.
